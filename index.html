It was a dark and stormy night...

The wind in the trees made strange noises while the two teenagers were walking home.

Shivering, Meredith glanced up and thought she saw the Cheshire Cat glowering at her from a tree branch.

"The queen is dead and gone", said the cat, smiling broadly. "At least gone. Long live Meredith!"

Meredith stopped dead in her tracks. "Wait—what—??"

"I don't want to be a queen," she said. "Queen of what?"

"Why, Wonderland," the Cheshire Cat said. "Haven't you been there recently?"

"Only in my dreams," said Meredith.

"That's how it's done," the Cheshire Cat said. "Don't you remember Alice?"

"Oh, right," Meredith said. "She was napping."

The Cheshire Cat nodded emphatically. "So are you ready, my dear? We must not keep the Red Queen waiting. After all, she wants you dead."
